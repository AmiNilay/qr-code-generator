Create your custom QR Code

1. Set QR Content

Select a content type from the tabs (URL, Text, Email, etc.). After selecting a type, enter all the required information in the fields below. Make sure your data is correct, as it cannot be changed after the QR code is generated.

2. Review the Design

This tool focuses on a clean, consistent modern design. While direct color or shape customization isn't available, the professional look ensures your QR code is highly scannable on any background.

3. Generate QR Code

Click the "Generate QR Code" button. Your code will instantly appear in the preview area. It's essential to test your QR code by scanning the preview image with your phone's camera or a QR scanner app to ensure it works as expected.

4. Download Image

Right-click (or long-press on mobile) on the generated QR code image and select "Save Image As..." to download it as a .PNG file. This high-quality format is perfect for web use, presentations, documents, and printing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are the 'live' stats calculated?+

Great question! Since this website runs entirely in your browser and doesn't use a central server (for privacy), these stats are a fun simulation. The 'Users Online' count fluctuates realistically, while 'Generated Today' and 'Total Generated' start at a base number and increase by one every time you create a QR code. This gives the site a dynamic feel while respecting your privacy completely.

How does 'Multi-URL' work?+

A QR code stores only one link. This tool creates a mobile-friendly webpage with all your links, and the QR code points to that page. It's a free and effective way to share multiple links.

Can I use a PDF file?+

QR codes can't store files. You must upload your PDF online (e.g., Google Drive), get the public link, and paste it in the 'PDF' tab.

What is a vCard?+

It's a digital business card. Scanning it prompts the user to save your details directly to their phone's contacts.
